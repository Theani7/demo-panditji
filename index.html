Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a pandit?

+

Simply click on "Book Now", select your service type, choose your preferred pandit, pick a date and time, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation within 2 hours.

Are all pandits verified?

+

Yes, all our pandits go through a rigorous verification process including background checks, experience validation, and customer feedback reviews before joining our platform.

What materials are included?

+

Basic puja materials like flowers, incense, oil lamps, and prasadam are included. For special ceremonies, we provide all required items as per the ritual requirements.

Can I reschedule my booking?

+

Yes, you can reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. Contact our support team for assistance.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

+

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll either send another pandit or provide a full refund within 48 hours.

Do you provide services outside major cities?

+

We currently serve 25+ cities across Nepal and are expanding rapidly. Check our service areas during booking or contact us for availability in your location.
